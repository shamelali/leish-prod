# Financial Projection Forecast — Leish! Beauty Booking Marketplace

## Executive Summary

This forecast projects 3-year financial performance for Leish!, a beauty booking marketplace connecting customers with makeup artists and studios in Malaysia.

---

## Key Assumptions

### Market Context
- **Target Market**: Malaysia beauty services market (~RM 2.8B annually)
- **Service Categories**: Bridal, Event, Photoshoot, SFX, Lessons, Hari Raya, CNY, Traditional Malay, Hijab
- **Platform Commission**: 15% per booking (market standard)
- **Average Booking Value**: RM 250 (bridal RM 800+, events RM 150-400)

## Revenue Streams

### 1. Commission Fees (Primary)
- **Rate**: 15% per booking
- **Year 1**: RM 307,500 (92.5% of revenue)
- **Year 2**: RM 2,025,000 (92.9% of revenue)
- **Year 3**: RM 8,250,000 (91.7% of revenue)

### 2. Premium Artist Subscriptions
- **Tier**: RM 99/month for featured placement
- **Adoption**: 10% of artists by Year 2
- **Year 2**: RM 120,000 (200 artists × 10% × RM 99 × 12 months)

### 4. Studio Partnerships

## Risk Factors

### High Impact Risks
1. **Market Competition**: Existing players (Booky, Qmule)
   - Mitigation: Niche focus on luxury/premium segment

2. **Artist Churn**: Artists bypassing platform
   - Mitigation: Escrow payments, value-added services

3. **Regulatory**: Payment escrow compliance
   - Mitigation: Legal review, Billplz partnership

4. **Seasonality**: 60% revenue in Q4 (wedding season)
   - Mitigation: Promotions in off-season, diversify categories

### Financial Risks
- **Payment fraud**: 0.5% chargeback rate assumed
- **Currency fluctuation**: USD infrastructure costs (Vercel, Neon)
- **Customer acquisition cost inflation**: Monitor CAC:LTV ratio
